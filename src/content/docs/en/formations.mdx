If you want the complete course today, start with the French version. If you are here to explore in English, use this page as a guide to the currently available EN content and the broader topics covered by WinFlowz.

## What the full training covers

The complete WinFlowz training is organized around eight themes:

### I. Productivity

Foundations, mindset, common traps, and how to define productivity in a way that serves your life.

### II. Windows

How to shape your operating system into a practical work environment.

### III. Time & Energy

How to protect deep work, structure your days, and work with your energy instead of against it.

### IV. Action Management

How to turn goals into projects, projects into tasks, and tasks into reliable execution.

### V. Consumption

How to filter content, reduce noise, and build a healthier information diet.

### VI. Knowledge

How to capture, organize, connect, and retrieve what you learn.

### VII. Social

How to manage communication, relationships, and collaboration without getting overwhelmed.

### VIII. Shortcuts

How to move faster with keyboard-first workflows and practical cheatsheets.
