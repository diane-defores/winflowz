From chaos to clarity: How Forerunner streamlined their operations
Founded in 2009 by visionary Kirsten Green, Forerunner Ventures has become a trailblazer in venture capital, reshaping the commerce landscape from its base in San Francisco. With a sharp focus on the evolving needs of the modern consumer, Forerunner partners with bold, innovative founders across B2B and B2C sectors, driving transformative growth. With a strong portfolio of 49 companies and a total fund of $658.6 million, it stands as a key influencer in shaping tomorrow's consumer landscape.


Headquarters

San Francisco

Founded

2012

Industry

Investment Management

Revenue

$5M

Company size

11-50

Challenge
Forerunner Ventures approached us with a critical challenge: their team was wasting valuable time navigating the chaos of scattered information across multiple tools. The endless search for key data and the growing pile of static documents were creating unnecessary friction and complexity in their daily operations.

They needed a solution that would centralize their workflows and establish a streamlined, organized system to support their fast-paced work environment.

The goal?

To transform their operations by creating a unified digital headquarters that would serve as the single source of truth for the entire organization.

Building Forerunner’s digital foundation
To tackle the issue, we started with one key question:

What does Forerunner need to work smarter, not harder?

We began by getting to know their team and their daily workflows. Through carefully designed surveys, we collected insights on everything from individual responsibilities to pain points across the organization. These surveys helped us uncover the nuances of Forerunner’s culture and operations.


Once the responses rolled in, we analyzed them and created a detailed audit document. This wasn't just a data dump; it was a strategic map that highlighted key challenges and prioritized them into four urgency levels, from P0 (critical) to P3 (low priority). By aligning our approach to these priorities, we laid a strong foundation for creating solutions that truly mattered to them.


Building Forerunner HQ
Armed with a clear understanding of their needs, we got to work building ForerunnerHQ, a centralized workspace designed to be their single source of truth.

Imagine a place where every team member knows exactly where to find the information they need — no more endless searches or tool-hopping. That’s what ForerunnerHQ set out to achieve. Using Notion as the backbone, we created a system where internal and external resources were categorized, meeting notes were easily accessible, and content planning was organized seamlessly. The workspace was built to be not only efficient but also mobile-friendly, ensuring that the team could work effectively, whether at their desks or on the go.


Seamless integration
To supercharge ForerunnerHQ, we didn’t just stop at organizing information — we automated processes.

We streamlined their project creation. When a new organization was added to Notion, automations kicked in to create a corresponding project in Affinity and a dedicated folder in Dropbox. These new resources were automatically linked back to the Notion database, so everything stayed connected and easy to find.

These integrations eliminated tedious manual updates, reduced tool-switching, and freed up the team to focus on what they do best — investing in innovative companies.


A tailored experience, with the user in mind
We wanted ForerunnerHQ to feel like a natural extension of their brand. From custom icons to a sleek, intentional interface, every detail was crafted to reflect Forerunner’s identity. This wasn’t just a workspace; it was a digital home that made the team feel proud and connected to their work.


Ongoing support, empowering the team
Creating a great system is only half the battle — it’s just as important to ensure the team knows how to use it.

To make the transition smooth, we built a dedicated Notion Training Hub within ForerunnerHQ. This space served as a go-to guide for onboarding new team members and helping everyone get up to speed on the new workflows. Clear documentation, how-to guides, and video tutorials ensured the team could hit the ground running without feeling overwhelmed.

Fawzi Itani

Investor, Forerunner Ventures

The results: A smarter, more efficient company
Through our collaboration, Forerunner Ventures gained more than just a digital workspace — they gained a culture of efficiency and innovation. Their team now spends less time searching for information and more time making strategic decisions. Processes are streamlined, operations are aligned, and their digital HQ is a foundation for future growth.

How Bain Capital Ventures Centralize their  Company Knowledge on Notion
Bain Capital Ventures, founded in 1984, is a prominent American private investment firm that has cemented its global presence across sectors and regions. With a staggering $165 billion of investor capital under management as of 2022, BCV is one of the world's leading private investment firms.


Headquarters

San Francisco, California

Founded

2001

Industry

Venture Capital and Private Equity Principals

Company size

51-200

Challenge
At the core of BCV digital transformation journey was recognizing the need to centralize company knowledge and enhance collaboration across applications. To achieve this, they hired us because they recognized the need for external expertise even though they already had internal intermediate-level Notion power users. One of the primary concerns for BCV was to minimize disruptions during the automation process.

We began by thoroughly analyzing BCV's existing workflows, tools, and data sources. This assessment helped identify pain points, inefficiencies, and areas for improvement. We interacted closely with BCV's team to better understand their needs and challenges.


Consulting Expertise and Guidance
Optimization's role in BCV's automation journey was that of a trusted advisor and guide. Our team of experienced consultants brought their deep knowledge of automation processes and best practices to the table, helping BCV understand the pros and cons of automating their information management system using Notion and Make.

Recognizing the importance of addressing all their questions and concerns regarding the automation process, our team created a comprehensive Notion database.

The Notion database served as a centralized repository, containing all the questions, inquiries, and doubts BCV had throughout its automation journey. By compiling this information in one place, our experts ensured nothing was overlooked or left unanswered.

The Notion database became a valuable resource for BCV, allowing them to access a wealth of information related to their automation initiative. It provided a structured and organized framework to address their queries and concerns, enabling efficient collaboration and knowledge sharing among stakeholders involved in the project.


Personalized Training and Skills Development
In addition to guiding the automation process, we also provided personalized training sessions to BCV's intermediate and advanced Notion users. Recognizing that efficient utilization of Notion would be crucial for optimal workflow management, our Certified Notion Consultants conducted one-on-one training sessions tailored to the specific needs of each user. These sessions equipped the users with the necessary skills and knowledge to navigate Notion effectively, helping them leverage its capabilities to their advantage.****

Documentation
Throughout the project, we maintained detailed documentation of the entire implementation process. This documentation included:

Workflow analysis and recommendations

Notion workspace structure and configurations

Integration and automation setup guides

Training materials and resources

Troubleshooting and support documentation

Conclusion
Through close collaboration with us, BCV successfully transformed how data was integrated across Notion and other apps. The project resulted in several key achievements:

Centralization of data and information in Notion -- eliminating the need for manual data entry and reducing information duplication

Streamlined processes and improved efficiency through automated data flow between systems

Timely access to relevant information and data insights, empowering the investment team to make informed decisions

Development of internal Notion power users who can maintain, support, and further enhance the system

Creation of comprehensive documentation for future reference and knowledge sharing

By leveraging our expertise, BCV achieved its vision of a streamlined, data-driven investor workflow. The optimized workflow enabled the investment team to focus on strategic decision-making and gain a competitive edge in the market.
